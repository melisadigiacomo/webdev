Best view of Cordoba

“ On the way there are 6 breaks. On the 5th there is shelter. If I remember correctly when you arrive at the refuge you are more or less in the middle of the journey. Up to that point the trail is pretty quiet without too much hassle, I would say pretty much anyone can get there. From there it gets a lot more demanding, it is steep and there are no more smooth sections. At the top the view is incredible so it is worth the effort. The descent is the hardest part. ”

Such incredible experience. Highly recommended!

“ It is a fascinating place with beautiful views, the climate is very changeable. The path to get there is very well defined, although it has medium complications on the ground, with patience and perseverance you reach the top. Good advise: bring water (2L at least) and light clothing. There are 6 breaks to the top. At the 5th break there is a refuge. From the 6th to the top the road is more complex. Try to go up as early as possible. We left the base at 7.30 am and we did most of the climb without any sun. The entrance is very expensive, and you have to pay for parking. With the entrance you have absolutely nothing included, it is only the permission to climb. Nevertheless, the experiences is incredible. I highly recommend! ”

Daniel Evans
